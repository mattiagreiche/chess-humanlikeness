Methods (brief)

Moves are scored by the probability Maia-2 assigns to the actual played move. Per-move scores are aggregated per player and per phase (opening/mid/end). Dimensionality reduction (PCA) on these aggregated features yields the dominant latent axis; robustness checks include top-k stylometry and correlation with Elo.
